Conditions We Treat in St. Peters, MO

Pain has a cause. Finding it is the first step toward fixing it. At Morning Star, we treat a range of nerve, joint, and spinal conditions using non-surgical therapies that target the problem at its source. Select a condition below to learn about causes, symptoms, and the treatment options available to you.

Neuropathy

Peripheral nerve damage causing tingling, numbness, burning pain, and balance problems in the hands and feet. Treated with neuromodulation, regenerative therapy, and customized nerve rehabilitation.

Knee Pain

Osteoarthritis, cartilage loss, and joint degeneration that limit mobility and daily activities. Treated with PRP therapy, hyaluronic acid injections, and regenerative therapy.

Back Pain

Herniated discs, degenerative disc disease, and chronic lower back pain that interferes with work and sleep. Treated with regenerative therapy, chronic pain management, and PRP therapy.

Neck Pain

Cervical strain, stiffness, and radiating pain from spinal conditions or postural damage. Treated with neuromodulation and chronic pain management.

Joint Pain

Osteoarthritis, joint inflammation, and progressive stiffness affecting shoulders, hips, and other joints. Treated with PRP therapy, hyaluronic acid injections, and regenerative therapy.

Sciatica

Sciatic nerve compression causing radiating leg pain, numbness, and weakness from the lower back. Treated with neuromodulation, chronic pain management, and regenerative therapy.

How We Approach Condition-Specific Care

Every condition has a different root cause, and every patient experiences it differently. That is why Morning Star does not use generic treatment templates. We start with a thorough evaluation of your specific condition, including your symptoms, medical history, and diagnostic findings. Then we build a treatment plan using the pain management and regenerative medicine therapies that match your situation.

The condition pages above explain what causes each problem, the symptoms to watch for, and the specific services we offer to treat them. If you are unsure which condition applies to your pain, schedule an evaluation and our team will help you find the answer.

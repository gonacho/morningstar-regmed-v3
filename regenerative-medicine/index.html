Regenerative Medicine at Morning Star

Regenerative medicine represents a shift away from treatments that only mask pain. Instead of covering up symptoms with medication, these therapies stimulate your body's natural repair mechanisms at the cellular level. Damaged cartilage, inflamed tendons, and deteriorating joints respond to treatments that deliver concentrated growth factors and signaling molecules directly to the injury site.

Our St. Peters clinic offers three targeted regenerative treatments, each suited to different conditions and stages of tissue damage. Every patient receives a thorough evaluation before we recommend which approach, or combination of approaches, fits their situation.

Regenerative Medicine Services

Regenerative Therapy (Exosomes)

Exosome-based treatments that deliver growth factors directly to damaged tissue, reducing inflammation and promoting cellular repair for long-term healing.

Platelet Rich Plasma Therapy

PRP injections concentrate your own platelets to accelerate healing in joints and soft tissues. Sessions take under an hour with minimal downtime.

Hyaluronic Acid Injections

Restore natural joint lubrication with minimally invasive HA injections. Relief typically lasts 4 to 6 months with results building over several weeks.

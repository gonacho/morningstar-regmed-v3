Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions does Morning Star treat?
Morning Star treats peripheral neuropathy, chronic pain, knee pain, back pain, neck pain, joint pain, and sciatica using non-surgical approaches including neuromodulation, regenerative therapy, PRP, and hyaluronic acid injections.
Where is Morning Star located?
Morning Star Neuropathy & Pain Treatment Center is located at 4710 Mexico Rd Suite C, St. Peters, MO 63376. We serve patients throughout St. Charles County and the greater St. Louis metropolitan area.
What are Morning Star's office hours?
Morning Star is open Monday through Thursday, 8am to 5pm. Call (636) 244-0124 or schedule online at any time.
Does Morning Star accept insurance?
Morning Star accepts major medical insurance. We also offer CareCredit financing. Contact our office for details about your specific plan.

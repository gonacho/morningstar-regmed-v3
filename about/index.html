About Morning Star Neuropathy & Pain Treatment Center

Helping patients in St. Peters and the greater St. Louis area find lasting relief from neuropathy and chronic pain through advanced, non-invasive care since 2009.

Our Story

Morning Star Neuropathy & Pain Treatment Center was founded to serve people in St. Peters, St. Charles County, and the greater St. Louis metropolitan area who had been told their only options were long-term medication, repeated cortisone injections, or surgery. That answer was not good enough.

We built a clinic around a different premise: advanced, non-invasive treatments that go after the actual source of pain. Regenerative therapies, neuromodulation, PRP, hyaluronic acid injections, and medically supervised weight loss were once limited to major academic medical centers. Morning Star brought them to a comfortable, patient-focused setting on Mexico Rd in St. Peters so that people across the region could access these treatments close to home.

We measure our success by the quality of life our patients reclaim: the grandmother who can walk to the mailbox again, the mechanic who returned to work without daily pain medication, the retiree who started golfing again after two years on the sideline. Those outcomes drive everything we do.

Our Treatment Philosophy

Every treatment decision at Morning Star starts with one question: what is causing this patient's pain? We believe lasting relief is only possible when the root cause is identified and addressed directly. That is why every new patient receives a thorough evaluation, including a health history review, neurological assessment, and diagnostic workup, before any treatment is recommended.

We combine advanced technologies like neuromodulation, platelet-rich plasma therapy, exosome-based regenerative therapy, and hyaluronic acid injections with individualized care plans tailored to each patient's condition, lifestyle, and goals. There is no one-size-fits-all protocol at this clinic.

We are committed to transparency at every step. Patients understand exactly what their treatment involves, why it was recommended, and what outcomes they can realistically expect.

Our Team

The clinical team at Morning Star includes experienced medical professionals who specialize in neuropathy, pain management, and regenerative medicine. Our practitioners hold advanced training in neuromodulation techniques, PRP therapy, exosome therapy, hyaluronic acid joint injections, and medically supervised weight management.

Continuous education is a cornerstone of our practice. Our providers regularly attend national conferences, complete advanced certifications, and stay current with peer-reviewed research. When new evidence supports a better approach, we adopt it.

Serving St. Peters and the Greater St. Louis Region

Our clinic is located at 4710 Mexico Rd, Suite C in St. Peters, near Mid Rivers Mall Drive, with easy access from I-70 and Highway 94. We welcome patients from across the region.

Insurance & Payment Options

We believe cost should not stand between you and effective pain treatment. Morning Star accepts major medical insurance plans and offers flexible payment options to make care accessible.

Insurance Coverage

Morning Star accepts most major medical insurance plans. Coverage varies by plan, service, and diagnosis. We recommend calling your insurance provider before your appointment to confirm benefits for the specific treatment being discussed.

Our front desk team is available to help you verify coverage and answer questions about your benefits. Call (636) 244-0124 and we will do our best to clarify what your plan covers before your first visit.

Note: Insurance coverage for regenerative therapies (PRP, exosome therapy, hyaluronic acid injections) and neuromodulation varies widely between plans. Some plans cover these services under specific diagnostic codes while others do not. We will help you understand your options.

Payment Options

CareCredit Financing

Morning Star accepts CareCredit, a healthcare financing option that allows you to spread the cost of treatment over time. CareCredit offers promotional financing periods and flexible monthly payment plans. Apply online or ask our front desk for details.

Direct Payment

We accept cash, check, and all major credit cards for patients paying out of pocket or covering copays and deductibles. Payment is due at the time of service unless a prior arrangement has been made.

Questions About Coverage?

If you are unsure whether your insurance covers a specific service, our team can help. Call us at (636) 244-0124 with your insurance information ready, and we will check your benefits on your behalf.

For new patients, we recommend verifying coverage before your first appointment. Bring your insurance card and photo ID to your visit so we can process claims promptly.

Do Not Let Cost Stop You From Getting Relief

Call (636) 244-0124 to discuss your insurance or payment options. Morning Star in St. Peters, MO is here to help.
